# Measure

> **Moved.** Measure is no longer an inspector section. The
> preferences retired from the inspector in s150 and now live in
> a toolbar popover.

## Where to find Measure now

The **Measure** tool's preferences live in a popover anchored to
the Measure button in the toolbar.

- **Use the tool:** activate **Measure** from the toolbox or
  press **M**. Tool behaviour — how to set the A and B points,
  how marquee selection of measurement nodes works, what the
  on-canvas annotations show, and how to copy a structured
  measurement block — is documented in **Measure** (4.6.3).
- **Tool preferences:** **right-click** the Measure button to
  open its popover.

The preferences round-trip with the document, same as before.

## Why this moved

Measure preferences are tool preferences, not document state.
Putting them next to the tool's toolbar button means you adjust
them when you reach for the tool, not when you're elsewhere in
the inspector.

## Where to next

- **Measure** (4.6.3) covers how to take measurements with the
  tool, including the click flow, marquee semantics,
  click-to-copy structured block, and on-canvas annotations.
- **Eyedropper** (4.6.1) and **Zoom** (4.6.2) are the other
